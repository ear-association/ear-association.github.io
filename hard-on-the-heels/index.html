Hard on the Heels - A Banda das Crechas

There's a little Celtic-y section in Pibroch that reminded me of Hard on The Heels by A Banda das Crechas, a Galician group from Spain. This was shared by an acquaintance who was intrigued by the rhythm. The track is a bit paradoxical to me - it seems to have a rather complex rhythm, but it still sounds like it's being played by friends in a pub. Far from academic! It's also the first time I ever pondered the origins of Celtic music.

Hard on the Heels was suggested by Mohan in response to Jethro Tull's Pibroch (Cap in Hand).

It reminded Andrew B. of Piperkovo Oro.

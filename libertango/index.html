Libertango - Ivano Battiston

The previous Balkan piece reminded me of this accordion performance which blows my mind every single time. Astor Piazzolla's tangos have a special place in my heart, and this soloist (Ivano Battiston) makes Libertango sparkle. An entire orchestra inside a single accordion.

Libertango was suggested by Eva in response to Taraf De Haidouks's Turceasca.

It reminded Jacob of Les covers de Persiflette's Zelda Chaconne.

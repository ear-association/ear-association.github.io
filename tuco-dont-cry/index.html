Tuco don't cry - Mariapepinos ft. Eggglub

I only played Zelda once, in the Ocarina of Time game the music is very beautiful. The cover of Zelda Chaconne made me think of this song by Mariapepinos (Ft. eggglub) where they take a melody from Zelda and create a beautiful song. I love this song and feel it should last much longer. It sounds like a perfect spring day walking in nature where you see many animals and find little treasures that you then take home for your private collection.

Tuco don't cry was suggested by Mati in response to Les covers de Persiflette's Zelda Chaconne.

It reminded Andrew of Blonde Redhead's Elephant Woman.
